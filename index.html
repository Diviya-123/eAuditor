Private Limited Company Incorporation

Private limited company is the most preferred form of entity for doing a business. It gives the entrepreneur several benefits. It gives the entrepreneur the right tools for growth, control, finance, succession, & protection. A private limited company is the best armour you can wear while stepping into the field of business. A private limited company was the choice of OLA, Swiggy, OYO, Paytm, Zomato, Flipkart and several other unicorns of the Indian startup ecosystem. These companies knew that in order to scale the heights they aim for; they need a private limited company.

The Benefits of Incorporating a Private limited company

Limited Liability

It Keeps the risk within the business and away from your personal life. Even if things go bad for your business, your personal assets will always be safe.

Unlimited Funding

You can invite unlimited amount of funds for your business. There is no restriction on the total capital one can raise for a private limited company. Moreover, people will be less reluctant while investing in a private limited company.

Separation of Ownership & Management

A private limited company can have a maximum of 200 owners, called shareholders, who contribute capital into the business. This gives private limited companies a huge scope to raise capital. But it will be a chaos if all these shareholders manage the business. Hence, the management can be different from the shareholders, called the Board of Directors. The directors work dedicatedly for the growth of the company. The directors can be removed/appointed by the shareholders.

Credibility & Trust

A private limited company enjoys significant credibility and trust among banks, customers, vendors and public at large. It is so because a private limited company has to operate under the laws framed by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). It also has to get its financials audited by an Independent Chartered Accountant and report from time to time to the MCA on its major operational and policy changes.

Preferred by Investors

Private limited company is the most preferred entity structure by venture capital investors, angel investors, government departments offering investment etc. Its easy to invest funds and equally easy to exit also. The credibility & trust that this form of business brings adds up to the investors’ confidence.

Easy Succession

A private limited company can be easily handed over to the next generation of shareholders and management. It has a perpetual succession. This is why we can find several companies with a history of more than 100 years, constantly growing and increasing its profits, assets and market share, building up their wealth year on year. A private limited company is also highly preferred by family businesses as it can forever be run by the family members generations after generations.

Opportunity to Get Listed

Listing on a stock exchange can change the story of any business. That’s because once listed, the business will have access to practically unlimited amount of funds. But, only a company can get listed.

Tax Benefits

The Government of India, in order to encourage its citizens to venture into businesses, launches schemes from time to time giving tax exemptions and tax cuts to newly formed companies.
